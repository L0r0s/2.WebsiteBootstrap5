Geschichte

SSC Napoli, also known as Napoli, is a professional football club based in Naples, Campania, Italy. Founded in 1926, the club has a rich history and has become one of the most successful and popular football clubs in Italy. Napoli has won several domestic and international titles, including Serie A, Coppa Italia, and the UEFA Cup. The club is known for its passionate fan base and its iconic stadium, Stadio Diego Armando Maradona, named after the legendary player who led the team to great success in the 1980s.

Decade by Decade

1920-1929

SSC Napoli was founded on August 1, 1926, by a merger of two local clubs. The club quickly established itself in Italian football and gained a loyal following.

1930-1939

In the 1930s, Napoli continued to grow in popularity and success, competing in Serie A and achieving respectable finishes in the league.

1940-1949

The 1940s were a challenging period for Napoli, as the club faced financial difficulties and the impact of World War II. Despite these challenges, the club managed to remain competitive.

1950-1959

In the 1950s, Napoli experienced a resurgence, winning the Coppa Italia in 1962 and establishing itself as a strong contender in Italian football.

1960-1969

The 1960s were a successful decade for Napoli, as the club won the Coppa Italia and achieved high finishes in Serie A. The club also began to attract top talent from around the world.

1970-1979

In the 1970s, Napoli continued to build on its success, winning the Coppa Italia and competing in European competitions. The club also moved to its current stadium, Stadio San Paolo, in 1959.

1980-1989

The 1980s were a golden era for Napoli, as the club won its first Serie A title in 1987, led by the legendary Diego Maradona. The club also won the Coppa Italia and the UEFA Cup during this period.

1990-1999

In the 1990s, Napoli continued to achieve success on the field, winning its second Serie A title in 1990. The club also faced financial difficulties and changes in ownership during this period.

2000-2009

The 2000s were a challenging period for Napoli, as the club faced relegation and financial difficulties. However, the club managed to rebuild and return to Serie A in 2007.

2010-2019

In the 2010s, Napoli experienced a resurgence, winning several domestic titles and competing in European competitions. The club also became known for its attacking style of play and talented players.

2020-Present

In the 2020s, Napoli continues to be one of the most successful and popular football clubs in Italy. The club remains committed to its attacking style of play and developing talented players.
